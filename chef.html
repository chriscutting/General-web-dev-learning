Chef Louis Marconi Bio

As you can see from our photo, Chef Louis Marconi is a classically trained Italian Chef that has been resposible for Louvino's fantastic menu since his employment began in 1992.


Born in the United States, Chef Louis practices the old ways of Italian cooking that were handed down to him from his father and his father before him.

Outside of work, chef Louis is a loving husband and father that practices photography and hangliding on the weekends. His recipe books are available for purchase, ask your server for more information.
