Welcome to Louvino's, home of Louisville's finest Italian cuisine. Located on River Road right next to the Ohio, Louvino's boasts the best carbonara in the city. Don't just take our word for it, Louvino's has been the subject of many local accolades and consistent praise on sites like Yelp and Tripadvisor.



Feel free to take a look around! We've got our menu available, online ordering set up, or just feel free to call us for a reservation. Louvino's also accommodates large parties with banquet seating available. Perfect for corporate events, birthdays, anniversaries, you name it. Come share your special day with us!
