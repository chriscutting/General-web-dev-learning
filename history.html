Louvino's - A brief history

Started by Sicilian immigrants that moved to Louiville, KY in 1856, Louvino's is the premier fine Italian dining experience in the city. How did we cultivate such a grand hypothetical reputation? Authenticity. Louvino's still uses the same family recipes brought over from the old country. If you want to experience true, authentic, Italian cooking in a beautiful, ambient environment, Louvino's is the place for you.
