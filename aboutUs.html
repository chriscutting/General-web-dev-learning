Louvino's

Louvino's is a family-owned and operated fine dining experience located on River Rd. in beautiful Louisville, KY. Our restaurant has been using Sicilian family recipes since its opening in 1912. With a rich history and well-built reputation for experience, Louvino's is committed to providing excellent experiences for all of its patrons. Louvino's accommodates all types of parties, from first dates to banquet seating for wedding receptions. Be sure to call ahead for reservations!


Vision:

Louvino's aims to maintain its current market position as one of the best locally-owned restaurant operations in the Louiville metro area. We strive to emulate an authentic experience. Louvino's aim to spread its current footprint from the Louisville metro area to the entire midwest, providing excellent dining experiences to a far greater crowd.


Mission:

Louvino's will maintain its current, superb quality of service by only using the freshest ingredients, authentic recipes, and by providing an unforgettable atmosphere. Louvino's pledges to always act in a customer-centric manner and offer reasonable prices for our dishes. At Louvino's, the customer comes first.
Remember: Louvino's is committed to you. If anything about your visit was unsatisfactory feel free to use the Contact us page on the navbar. Chef Rossi himself oversees our kitchen departments while world-renowned front-of-house expert Dan Smith helms our waitstaff. Feel free to use the contact form on the navbar for any suggestions we can improve upon. Please allow 3-5 business days for responses to inquiries.
